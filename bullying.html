Bullying

Different kinds of bullying

Verbal Bullying

Verbal bullying is when some one calls you bad names and hurt your feelings by speaking like "YOU ARE A LOSER".

Physical bullying

Physical bullying is when someone hurts you by touching. Its when mostly the bullies do it in front of people to show off.

Cyber Bullying

Cyber Bullying happens through the internet in online. Its now one of the major bullying happening.It happens when someone hacks into your account and post something which you wanted to keep private or when someone posts rude messages about you.
